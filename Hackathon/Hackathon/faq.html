FAQ



How do I use this app?

After you attach your sensor to your plants, you need to connect it to your device or you can also can connect to a Zigbee Gateway. Afterwards you can easily enter the ID number
(given on the box)with your email address and password in our website. Then you have to select that specific sensor from the “Add New Plant” page which can be chosen by the ID number.

What shall I do if my plant sensor stopped working, can I have a refund in case of my sensors are not working?

You can contact us by phone or via E-mail. We will make sure to detect problems as soon as possible . Please read our return policy to make sure you return the item in the given time phase.

Why should I use this product? I can take care of my plant without paying for something that is double the amount of it.

By using our sensors you can make sure that you are giving the right amount of care to your plant. Nothing less ,nothing more. The only thing you have to do is check the website or our app time to time.

What if my plant dies even if I keep checking the website and sensors regularly?

We can guarantee you that our sensors are sensitive enough to detect any small changes that the plants can have. But if you want to have a more professional care we recommend you
to try our “Professional” option instead of “Community” option. You can make sure to control more environmental factors (soil , humidity level etc.) .
